Bio

I hold a B.Eng degree in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering with second class upper honors from the University of Ilorin , where I was supervised by Professor I. I. Ahmed.

As a member of the ML Collective, I'm conducting independent research on leveraging Machine Learning (ML), particularly Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), to enhance and optimize Additive Manufacturing (3D-Printing) processes and material design. Additionally, I serve as a Metallurgist/Materials Engineer at Innov8 Startup Hub in Abuja, Nigeria, where I apply my expertise to explore innovative solutions at the intersection of materials science and artificial intelligence.
